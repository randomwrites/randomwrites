Coming Out as Bisexual (Sawyer Version)

Welcome to the journey of coming out as bisexual. This website is a safe space to explore and understand your feelings, and to find support and resources.

Remember, coming out is a personal process, and you should do it in your own time and way.

Resources

  • The Trevor Project - A leading national organization providing crisis intervention and suicide prevention services to LGBTQ+ youth.
  • Human Rights Campaign - Working for a world where everyone can live free from discrimination regardless of who they are or who they love.
  • Bisexual Resource Center - Resources and support for the bisexual community.

Share Your Story

If you feel comfortable, share your coming out story with us. Your experiences can inspire and help others on their journey.
